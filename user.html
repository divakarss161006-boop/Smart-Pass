The Sri Shakthi Institute of Engineering and Technology Smartpass Digital Management System (Smartpass-DMS) is a dedicated online platform developed to simplify and digitalize campus management processes.

SIET - Smartpass-DMS

  • Real-time data access for students, faculty, and administrators to track records and updates instantly.
  • Digital leave and hostel management that eliminates paperwork and streamlines approval processes.
  • Feedback and grievance handling system to enhance communication between students and staff.
  • A modern, responsive interface designed for smooth navigation across all devices.
